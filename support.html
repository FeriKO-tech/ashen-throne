Account
How do I reset my password? +
Visit the Account page and click “Forgot your oath?”. Enter your registered email address and we’ll send a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn’t arrive.
Can I change my username? +
Usernames can be changed once every 90 days from your Account settings page. Note that your old username becomes immediately available to other players once changed.
How do I enable two-factor authentication? +
2FA can be enabled in Account Settings under Security. We support authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS verification. We strongly recommend authenticator apps for account security.
I was banned — what do I do? +
If you believe the ban was issued in error, submit a ban appeal via support@ironveilstudios.com with your account username and a detailed description of the situation. Appeals are reviewed within 5 business days.
Technical
The launcher won’t open — what should I try? +
Run the launcher as Administrator, ensure your firewall isn’t blocking it, and verify your .NET runtime is up to date. If issues persist, reinstall via the download page. Detailed installation logs are found at %AppData%\IronveilStudios\logs.
I’m experiencing high latency in game. +
Check our server status on the News page. Try selecting a closer server region in Settings › Connection. A wired Ethernet connection is strongly recommended over Wi-Fi for competitive PvP play.
How do I report a bug? +
Use the in-game bug report button (F11) for immediate capture, or email bugs@ironveilstudios.com with your system specs, a description of the issue, and any reproduction steps.
What are the minimum system requirements? +
OS: Windows 10 64-bit  ·  CPU: Intel i5-8400 / AMD Ryzen 5 2600  ·  RAM: 8 GB  ·  GPU: GTX 1060 6 GB / RX 580  ·  Storage: 40 GB SSD recommended. Visit the download page for full recommended specs.
Gameplay
How does the PvP flagging system work? +
Outside designated safe zones, any player can be flagged for PvP. Entering contested territories automatically flags you. Unflagging has a 30-second cooldown. Players in safe zones cannot be attacked regardless of flag status.
How do I join a guild? +
Open the Guild panel (G key), browse available guilds by name or tag, and send a join request. Guild leaders and officers can accept or decline applications. You can also be directly invited by an officer.
What happens when I die in PvP? +
You lose a portion of your equipped gear’s durability and drop a small percentage of carried resources. Death never causes permanent item loss unless you are carrying cursed loot marked with a skull icon.
How do I access the Voidrift region? +
Complete the “Rift Seeker” quest chain (available at level 45+) to unlock the Voidrift portal in Veilhaven. The zone scales to your level, so it remains challenging regardless of when you first enter.
