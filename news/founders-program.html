The Founder's Program Is Now Open

Before the throne is contested, before the first siege is called, before the ash settles — some will have already chosen their side. The Founder's Program is for those people. Your mark on the Shattered Realm starts here.

January 15, 2026 Announcement Early Access ~7 min read
Program Status

The Founder's Program is open now. All three tiers are available while slots remain. Tier availability is not publicly displayed — when a tier closes, it closes permanently. There will be no second window.

What Is the Founder's Program?

The Ashen Throne Founder's Program is a pre-launch recognition program created for players who want to be part of Ashen Throne from the beginning — not as testers, not as backers in the traditional sense, but as permanent members of the game's opening chapter. Founders are the first warriors in the Shattered Realm. Their presence during launch shapes the initial political landscape, the founding guild alliances, and the server histories that will be referenced in the game's community for years.

There are three tiers. Each tier exists on a different philosophical premise about what it means to be a founder. The free tier — Ash Warden — requires no purchase and is available to anyone who registers an Ashen Throne account before the launch date. The paid tiers — Ironbound and Crimson Lord — include early access windows, prestige cosmetics, and permanent account designations that cannot be earned or purchased after launch, ever.

We are not offering pay-to-win advantages. Every piece of gear available in the game will be obtainable through gameplay. What Founders receive is recognition, early presence, and cosmetics that will always signal that you were there before the rest of the world arrived.

The Three Founder Tiers

Each tier builds on the previous. Ironbound includes everything in Ash Warden. Crimson Lord includes everything in Ironbound and Ash Warden.

Non-Negotiable Limitation

The cosmetics, titles, portrait frames, and account designations in all three Founder tiers will never be available again after the Founder's Program closes. This is a permanent design decision. We will not reissue Founder items in response to demand, future events, or any other circumstances. What Founders carry, they carry alone.

Early Access — What It Means

Early access in the Founder's Program is not a beta. When early access begins, the game is live. Servers are permanent. Progress carries forward. There are no wipes, no rollbacks, no experimental builds. Early access is simply first access to the live game before the public launch date.

Ironbound Founders receive 48-hour early access. The Ironbound window opens two days before the official public launch date, giving Ironbound Founders the first opportunity to establish characters, begin factional allegiances, and claim territory before the world population arrives.

Crimson Lord Founders receive 72-hour early access — one additional day before Ironbound access opens. In practice, this means Crimson Lord Founders have three full days to embed themselves in the Realm's opening chapter before any non-Founder reaches the game. Guild leaders, strategy communities, and competitive players have historically found this window to be the highest-value element of premium founder programs.

What Can You Accomplish in 72 Hours?

In early playtesting data, a focused player can reach Level 35 (out of 60 at launch) in roughly 60–70 hours of play. A Crimson Lord with 72 hours of uncontested access can realistically establish a character, complete the first two faction storyline chapters, secure early-game resource nodes, and found a guild before the Ironbound window opens. A Crimson Lord guild that coordinates its 72-hour window has historically — in comparable MMOs — established territorial advantages that persist for the first two weeks of general population play.

This is not guaranteed. The Shattered Realm is unpredictable by design. But the window matters.

Character Name Reservation

Ironbound Founders may reserve one character name through the Founder's Portal before any server is live. Crimson Lord Founders may reserve three names. Reserved names are locked to your account and cannot be claimed by any other player.

Name reservations open 48 hours before early access begins. You will receive an email notification with the reservation portal link when the window opens. Reserved names follow the same naming rules as the live game: 3–20 characters, no special characters, no impersonation of Ironveil Studios staff or official game entities.

If a reserved name violates naming policy upon review, you will be notified and given 24 hours to select an alternative before early access launches.

Cosmetic Details

Ironbound Plate Set

A full cosmetic armor set constructed around the visual language of cold iron and ancient war. Matte dark-grey plating with hand-hammered texture, bronze rivets, and a sigil motif referencing pre-Collapse Caldenmere craftsmanship. Available for all 8 classes with class-appropriate silhouette adjustments. No visual effects on the base set — the design philosophy was restraint over excess.

Founder's Blade Weapon Skin

A single-weapon-slot cosmetic skin available in class-appropriate forms: a straight sword for Warden and Dreadknight, twin daggers for Shadowblade, a greatsword for Ironclad, a staff for Arcanist, a mace for Oathsinger, a bound tome for Voidcaller, and a longbow for Ashwalker. The skin applies a subtle engraved-rune effect along the blade or shaft. Passive glow effect can be toggled off in cosmetic settings.

Crimson Sovereign Armor Set

The highest-tier cosmetic in the Founder's Program. Deep crimson and brushed-obsidian plating with animated golden-ember shoulder and chest pieces. A crown-motif helm with optional visor display. All class variants retain the signature crimson-and-obsidian colour language while adapting the silhouette to match the class archetype. This set was designed by lead character artist Mira Vael specifically for the Founder's Program and has no visual precedent in the existing game's art direction.

Crimson Throne Mount

A construct mount — not a living creature — built in the aesthetic of a throne carried by four spectral ember-bearers. Walking speed is normal. Flying speed is normal. What distinguishes it is the visual gravity it carries: the mount cannot be mistaken for anything other than a Crimson Lord's designation. Custom arrival and departure animations. The ember-bearers extinguish when the mount is dismissed. No sound effects that would create in-game advantages in PvP zones.

Hall of Founders

Crimson Lord Founders will have their character names permanently displayed in the Hall of Founders — an in-game location within Veilhaven's central archive tower that becomes accessible to all players at launch. The Hall is a navigable space with each name engraved on an obsidian panel alongside the date and server of their first login. It is not an interactive landmark in gameplay terms. It is simply a record.

How to Claim Your Founder Status

The process is three steps and takes less than five minutes:

  1. Create your Ashen Throne account at ashenthrone.com/account. Ash Warden status is automatically applied to any account created while the Founder's Program is active.
  2. Download the launcher from ashenthrone.com/download. The launcher is where you will redeem your Founder tier, manage your character names, and access early server windows when they open.
  3. Upgrade to Ironbound or Crimson Lord (optional) through the Founder's Portal within the launcher. Cosmetics and tier designation are applied to your account immediately upon completion.

All Founder-tier rewards are applied to your account wardrobe automatically. They are available on any character you create on any server, past or future. There is no redemption code, no one-time use restriction, and no expiry date on cosmetic access.

Cross-Server, Cross-Character

Every Founder reward — cosmetics, titles, frames, and the Hall of Founders inscription — is attached to your Ironveil Studios account, not to a single character or server. Transfer servers, create new characters, return after years away: your Founder status follows you everywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Founder's Program pay-to-win?

No. The XP boost included in Ironbound and Crimson Lord is time-limited (30 days from first activation) and affects only the speed at which you level, not the ceiling. No stat bonuses, no gear advantages, no currency. Every piece of gear in Ashen Throne is obtainable through gameplay. Founders have a head start. Not a permanent advantage.

When does the Founder's Program close?

The Ash Warden tier closes at the moment public access begins. Ironbound and Crimson Lord tiers will close before then — potentially significantly before then if uptake reaches internal limits. We will announce closures with a minimum of 72-hour notice via email to registered accounts and through the official social channels. There will be no countdown timer visible on the site.

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Yes, you can upgrade from Ash Warden to Ironbound, or from Ironbound to Crimson Lord, at any point while those tiers remain open. You pay only the difference between tiers. Once a tier closes, the upgrade path to that tier is no longer available.

What happens if I create my account after the Founder's Program closes?

You play the game in full and have access to every piece of gameplay content ever released. You simply will not carry the Founder's designation or the exclusive cosmetics. The game was not designed for Founders only — it was designed for everyone. The program exists to recognize those who showed up first.

Will Founder cosmetics ever appear in the in-game store?

No. Not in a future season, not in a throwback event, not in any form. The cosmetics and titles in the Founder's Program are permanently exclusive. This is the same commitment we made to Early Access communities in previous titles and it was kept without exception.

Your Move

The Shattered Realm is waiting. The war has not started yet. Register now to claim Ash Warden status for free. Or download the launcher and upgrade to Ironbound or Crimson Lord through the Founder's Portal. When the gates open, you will already be standing at the threshold.
