Season of Embers

For twenty-one days, the Shattered Realm burns with purpose. Ancient flame stirs in the Pyremark depths. The gods left fire behind for a reason. Now it is yours to claim.

March 18, 2026 Event Limited Rewards ~5 min read
Event Dates

Season of Embers runs April 1 through April 21, 2026. All event content and daily quests begin at 00:00 UTC on April 1 and end at 23:59 UTC on April 21. Rewards can be claimed up to 48 hours after the event closes.

The Ember Prophecy

Three thousand years ago, when the last of the gods fell, their divine essence did not simply vanish. The scholars of the Ashen Crown have long believed that divine fire — the primal energy of creation and destruction that the gods wielded as casually as breathing — seeped into the earth beneath the Pyremark highlands and lay dormant, waiting.

Every year, for exactly twenty-one days, that fire rises. Not in catastrophic eruption, but in a slow, controlled pulse — as if the earth itself is breathing out. The Pyremark glows differently during the Season of Embers. The volcanic vents run hotter. The sky carries a residual haze of ember-orange that has no meteorological explanation.

The three factions have historically used this window to press their territorial claims in the Pyremark highlands. The Ember Season is war accelerated. Every kill counts more. Every keep held during these twenty-one days carries lasting territorial weight into the next quarter. And this year, something beneath the Pyremark has awoken that was not awake last year.

How to Participate

The Season of Embers is open to all players Level 20 and above. No special quest chain or pre-requirement is needed to begin participation. Log in during the event period and you will receive the Ember Seal currency in your inventory automatically. From that point, everything in the event runs through the Ember progression system.

  • Complete Ember Tide daily quests to earn Ember Seals. There are three daily quests per day, each designed to take 15–25 minutes. All three can be done solo or in groups.
  • Participate in Ember Spire dungeon runs for accelerated Seal gain and exclusive dungeon-only loot.
  • Join your guild in the Ashen Colossus world event for server-wide reward milestones and the event's highest-tier cosmetic unlocks.
  • All Ember Seals carry over between days but expire when the event ends. Use them before April 21.

Ember Tide Daily Quests

Three new daily quests become available each reset at 00:00 UTC. They rotate through a pool of twelve total quests across the 21-day event. Each quest takes place across the Pyremark, Ashfeld, and Veilhaven zones and is designed to be completable without a premade group, though groups can complete them faster.

  • Tide of Ash — Defeat elite ember-touched creatures in the Pyremark southern vents. Rewards are scaled to your gear tier.
  • The Merchant's Fire — Escort ember-ore shipments from the Pyremark mines to Veilhaven's western docks through contested territory. PvP-enabled route.
  • Remnants of the Divine — Investigate glowing rune sites across Ashfeld and collect divine ember fragments. Instanced for non-PvP players; open-world version available for bonus Seals.

The Ember Spire Dungeon

A new limited dungeon — the Ember Spire — opens exclusively during the Season of Embers. It does not appear in the dungeon finder outside the event window.

The Ember Spire is a four-player dungeon set inside a Pyremark volcanic tower that has been hollowed out over centuries by ember-touched cultists who worshipped the sleeping divine fire. It is not a long dungeon — a prepared group will clear it in 35–50 minutes — but it is built around mechanics that require all four roles to function. The final boss, Verak the Ember-Touched, is a former Dreadknight warlord who absorbed too much divine fire during the last Ember Season and did not survive the transformation intact.

The Ember Spire has two difficulty tiers. Normal tier is accessible immediately at Level 20. Heroic tier unlocks at 3,000 Ember Seals and requires a pre-formed group of four.

Ember Spire Exclusive Loot

The Ember Spire drops gear that cannot be obtained elsewhere during or after the event: the Ashen Covenant weapon set and the Pyremark Commander title. Both are cosmetic in nature and carry no stat bonuses.

The Ashen Colossus — Server-Wide World Event

Every three days during the Season of Embers, the Ashen Colossus awakens in the central Pyremark plateau. This is a server-wide open-world encounter that requires coordinated participation from across the entire active server population to complete. It is not structured as instanced content — any player in the Pyremark zone contributes automatically when the encounter is active.

The Colossus has six encounter phases, each unlocked by the server reaching damage thresholds. Phases escalate dramatically in intensity, and the final phase — Divine Conflagration — releases an AoE that covers the entire plateau and will eliminate any player not inside the shield circles maintained by Oathsinger-class players.

Players who contribute to the killing blow phase receive Ember Seals ×300 and are eligible for the Colossus Loot Pool, which includes the Season's highest-rarity cosmetics. Contribution is tracked individually — there is no minimum damage threshold to qualify, only minimum participation time (at least 5 minutes in the zone while the encounter is active).

The Colossus will rise on: April 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, and 21 at 20:00 UTC each night.

Season Rewards

All rewards below are earned through Ember Seal accumulation. Seals are spent at the Ember Emissary NPC who appears in all capital cities and in the Pyremark staging area for the duration of the event. Nothing in the Season of Embers reward pool requires real-money purchase.

Limited Availability

The Ember Drake Mount and Cinderplate Armor Set will not return to the game after April 21, 2026. Previous seasons' exclusive rewards have never been reissued. If you want them, this is your only window.

Guild Objectives

Guilds with 10 or more active members during the Season of Embers unlock a separate set of Guild Ember Objectives — server-visible milestones that contribute to a per-guild seasonal ranking. The top three guilds on each server at the end of April 21 earn the Ember Vanguard guild banner that persists as a visible trophy in their guild hall for the following season.

Guild Objectives include territory control milestones in the Pyremark, cumulative Ashen Colossus contribution across all guild members, and an optional guild-vs-guild siege event in the Ember Flats — a sub-zone of the Pyremark plateau that opens exclusively during the Season for faction-aligned guild warfare.

Guild Objective progress is visible to all guild members via the Guild Panel's new Seasonal tab, which will appear automatically when the event begins.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I earn the Ember Drake on a new character?

Yes. A Level 20 character can earn enough Ember Seals through daily quests and the world event over 21 days to reach the 6,000 Seal threshold without ever completing Heroic content. The maths work out to approximately 285 Seals per day across all three daily quests — slightly more if you participate in Colossus events.

Does the event run on all servers simultaneously?

Yes. The Season of Embers runs on EU (Ashfeld), NA (Pyremark), and Asia-Pacific (Voidrift) servers with synchronized start and end times based on UTC. There is no region-exclusive content.

What happens to Ember Seals I didn't spend?

All unspent Ember Seals expire 48 hours after the event closes on April 21. They cannot be carried forward to future seasons or converted to any other currency. Spend them before April 23 at 23:59 UTC.

Are the Blazebound weapon skins account-wide?

Yes. All cosmetic items earned during the Season of Embers are applied to your account's cosmetics wardrobe and can be applied to any current or future character on the account, regardless of class.
