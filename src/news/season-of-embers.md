## What Is the Season of Embers?

Once a year, the volcanic highlands of the Pyremark region experience what locals call the Ember Tide — a three-week period when the underground magma chambers breach the surface at predictable fault lines, and the ancient fire-bound creatures sealed beneath the earth stir awake. Ironveil Studios marks this in-world event with the Season of Embers: a limited-time content wave with exclusive cosmetics, a new dungeon, and seven rotating world boss encounters.

Everything earned during the Season of Embers is permanently available to your account once acquired. Nothing expires. But the window to acquire it closes on April 21 — and these cosmetics will never return to the shop or any other in-game source.

## Exclusive Seasonal Rewards

All rewards listed here are **permanent unlocks** once earned. They will not reappear in future seasons, the shop, or any other content.

### Emberveil Drake (Mount)
The signature reward of this season. A fire-elemental drake with an animated ember particle trail, custom mount ability (Ash Cloud — drops a slowing smoke screen), and a unique mount sound set. Obtained by completing the Cinderhold Dungeon on Heroic difficulty at least once before the season ends.

### Cinderplate Armor Set (All Classes)
A full cosmetic armor set with individual pieces earned through Ember Point accumulation. Each class has a unique visual interpretation of the Cinderplate design. Requires 4,000 Ember Points total to unlock the complete set.

### Blazebound Weapon Skin Pack
A set of 8 weapon skins — one per class — with animated fire effects on abilities. Obtained by defeating each of the three world bosses at least once. Tracked per account; partial completion does not grant the pack.

### Cinderplate Title: "The Ember-Touched"
Account-wide title. Awarded for completing the Seasonal Journal (see below). Displayed in all PvP arenas and social hubs.

### Seasonal Portrait Frame
Awarded automatically at season end to all players who logged in at least once during the season window.

## Ember Points System

Ember Points (EP) are the seasonal currency. They do not carry over to future seasons.

**How to earn EP:**
- Complete daily Ember Tasks: 250 EP each, 3 available per day
- Kill Ashen Colossus (weekly, per zone): 600 EP each
- Clear Cinderhold Dungeon: 400 EP per run (Normal), 800 EP per run (Heroic), once per week per difficulty
- Participate in a siege during Season of Embers event hours: 150 EP
- Guild Event: 800 EP for ranking in the top 10 in your server's Ember Guild Leaderboard

**EP Shop items:**
- Cinderplate Helmet: 800 EP
- Cinderplate Chest: 1,000 EP
- Cinderplate Legs: 800 EP
- Cinderplate Gloves: 400 EP
- Cinderplate Boots: 400 EP (600 EP total unlocks for remaining cosmetics)
- Ember Mount variant (lesser): 1,200 EP (not the Emberveil Drake — a separate cosmetic)

## Cinderhold Dungeon

The Cinderhold is a five-boss dungeon set entirely underground in the Pyremark fault system. It is available to groups of 1–5 players on Normal difficulty, and requires a group of exactly 4–5 players on Heroic difficulty (the Emberveil Drake source).

### Dungeon Overview

The Cinderhold follows the path of the original dwarven smelter guilds who first attempted to harness the Pyremark's volcanic energy three centuries ago. What they created still runs — and it still has defenders.

**Boss 1 — The Smelt Warden**
An automaton constructed from scrap iron and volcanic crystal, guarding the forge entrance. Mechanics: Magma Geyser (positional), Forge Slam (tank buster), Heat Overload (group cooldown check).

**Boss 2 — Twinflame Adherents**
Two linked bosses that must be killed within 15 seconds of each other. If one dies with the other above 10% HP, both reset. Requires coordination across two tanks and tightly managed DPS distribution.

**Boss 3 — The Cinder Serpent**
Environmental boss fight in a partially flooded lava channel. The floor periodically becomes impassable. Players must navigate rising lava while managing a DoT-heavy serpent with high mobility.

**Boss 4 — Vault Keeper Molthen**
A heavily armored vault guardian whose armor must be stripped using the environment's forge mechanisms before meaningful damage can be applied. The mechanics reward players who explored the dungeon thoroughly.

**Boss 5 (Heroic only) — The Pyremark Heart**
A manifestation of the Pyremark's ancient consciousness. Raid-style encounter with phase transitions, a soft enrage timer, and a full wipe mechanic at 30% HP if the environmental control mechanism isn't completed. This is the encounter that drops the Emberveil Drake.

## World Boss Encounters

The three Ashen Colossus variants are open-world encounters that spawn at fixed locations in the Pyremark region. They require approximately 30–60 players to defeat efficiently and are active during the schedule listed above.

All three encounters drop Ember Points, seasonal crafting materials, and a chance at Blazebound weapon skin fragments (5 fragments per skin; fragments are account-bound and persist if the season ends before you've assembled the full set — but cannot be completed afterward).

## Seasonal Journal

The Seasonal Journal is a checklist of 20 challenges unlocked automatically at season start. Completing all 20 awards the "The Ember-Touched" title.

Notable challenges include:
- Defeat all three world bosses at least once
- Complete Cinderhold on both Normal and Heroic
- Earn 3,000 EP through daily tasks alone (no boss/dungeon EP counted)
- Win a siege during Season of Embers event hours
- Reach maximum level on a character created after April 1 (Season Fresh Challenge)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I earn Ember Points on multiple characters?**  
Yes. Ember Points are tracked per account, not per character. All EP from all characters pool together in the Ember Market.

**What happens to unused Ember Points when the season ends?**  
They are deleted. There is no carry-over mechanism. We recommend spending all EP before April 21.

**Is the Emberveil Drake available in the shop?**  
No. The Emberveil Drake is only obtainable by completing the Cinderhold Dungeon on Heroic difficulty during the Season of Embers. It will not appear in the Ironveil Emporium now or in the future.

**Will Season of Embers return next year?**  
We do not comment on future seasonal content plans. Assume this is your only opportunity.
